About iglü

The Plugin Marketplace is the official site for discovering and installing verified plugins for the iglü Smart Home System, letting you do more with your iglü.

Submitting Plugins

You can develop your own plugins and submit them to the iglü marketplace. Check out the the iglü SDK to get started. If you are having any issues feel free to contact plugins@nacdlow.com for help.
